About

We are a group of citizens concerned for the welfare of our homeless neighbors.

Join us in the fight to end homelessness!

We organized revitilization projects in every state in order to bring current homeless shelters to unprecendented living standards

We are at the forefront of pushing for ground breaking ceremonies in every part of the country where homelessness is not addressed

We organize clothing drives which become the source of quality second-hand clothes for the homeless

We collect non-perishable food items for distribution to local food banks

We network with others organizations who are in-line with our cause

We provide warm meals to those who have no where else to go
